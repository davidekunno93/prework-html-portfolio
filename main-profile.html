Birth place

London town

Most people think Big Ben, the London Eye, high fasion and sophistication when they think of London. I lived here for 21 years and what stayed with me was the street culture. Strangers constantly walking past each other, waiting for a bus or train, sitting next to each other, grabbing delicious meaty foods on a bargain sharing the odd nod and moving on but also ready to jump into a bantering session at a moment's notice.

Heritage

Nigeria

The land I am originally from but have never visited. I always said I would but some years ago when given the chance I let my mother talk me out of it because she was afraid it would be too dangerous. People from my country are very tough minded, and thick-skinned. I'd like to think some of that rigor has been smuggled into my DNA and I can embody that for my kids to learn. My fellow country men are truly strong people, with a beautiful insight to life that you'll catch glimpses of when they joke around, make music, and dance to it.
